The Beaver Builder Theme uses the WordPress Customizer to set all of its theme options. The Customizer provides a live preview of your changes as you make them.

## Access the Customizer

You can access the Customizer in either of the following ways:

- On the WordPress admin panel, click **Appearance > Customize**
- Click **Customize** in the admin bar

![Opening the WordPress Customizer](/img/open-the-wordpress-customizer-199e2a37.jpg)

## Customizer tabs

The BB Theme adds several tabs to the WordPress Customizer:

### General

Configure layout settings, background colors and images, accent colors, typography for headings and text, button styles, and social links.

[View General tab settings](/bb-theme/customizer-settings/general)

### Header

Set up your site header including top bar, header layout, logo, and navigation menu styles.

[View Header tab settings](/bb-theme/customizer-settings/header)

### Content

Configure settings for your blog layout, archive pages, post layout, WooCommerce integration, and lightbox options.

[View Content tab settings](/bb-theme/customizer-settings/content)

### Footer

Customize footer widgets and main footer layout and styling options.

[View Footer tab settings](/bb-theme/customizer-settings/footer)

### Presets

Choose from pre-designed color schemes and layout combinations to quickly style your site.

### Code

Add custom CSS and JavaScript code globally to your site.

### Settings

Configure site identity (title, tagline, icon) and static front page options.

## Using the Customizer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a section">
    Click on any of the tabs in the left panel to expand and view available settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Adjust settings using the various controls. The preview on the right updates in real-time.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview on different devices">
    Use the device icons at the bottom of the Customizer panel to preview your site on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    When you're satisfied with your changes, click the **Publish** button at the top of the panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Changes in the Customizer are not saved until you click the **Publish** button. You can click the **X** at the top to exit without saving.
</Note>

## Responsive preview

The Customizer includes device preview icons at the bottom of the panel:

- **Desktop** - Preview your site at full width
- **Tablet** - Preview your site at medium device widths
- **Mobile** - Preview your site at small device widths

Many BB Theme settings are responsive, meaning you can configure different values for different device sizes.
