Sign up for the Assistant Pro Cloud at [Assistant.Pro](https://assistant.pro/). There are three Assistant Pro Cloud account types: Free, Individual, and Team. Compare prices and features on this [comparison table](https://assistant.pro/#pricing).

<Tip>
You can change your account type from **Account > Billing Information** on the Assistant Pro Cloud dashboard.
</Tip>

## Create account

You sign up for the Assistant Pro Cloud at [Assistant.Pro](https://assistant.pro/).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the Sign-Up Free button">
    Navigate to the Assistant Pro website and click the **Sign-Up Free** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill out the registration form">
    Complete all required fields in the registration form.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Use the request for verification you receive by email to verify your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete registration">
    Click the **Verify Email Address** button to complete your registration.
  </Step>
</Steps>
